# Early Repayment

You can pay off a ClickPe Lite loan earlier than its scheduled duration at any time, and there is no penalty or fee for doing so. Partial prepayments are allowed — you can pay any amount above the regular daily debit whenever you choose.

Early repayment reduces the total number of remaining daily debits but does not change the daily repayment amount already set for the loan. The excess you pay is applied to the principal outstanding.

If you prepay the full remaining balance, the loan is closed and the daily debit stops. The closed loan remains visible in your account history as a successful loan — which is good for future borrowing decisions.
