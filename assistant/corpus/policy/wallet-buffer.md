# Recommended Wallet Buffer

We recommend keeping at least three days worth of daily repayment in your wallet at all times. If your daily repayment is Rs 200, the recommended buffer is Rs 600.

A three-day buffer gives you cushion against normal day-to-day income variability: a slow Wednesday, a rainy afternoon, a delayed customer payment. Without a buffer, a single bad day can cascade into a skipped debit, and a few skipped debits can flip your loan into the defaulted state.

Your current buffer in days is calculated as (wallet balance) divided by (daily repayment). If this number drops below three, the risk engine flags you as MEDIUM risk. If it drops below one — meaning you cannot cover even tomorrow's debit — it flags HIGH.
