# Insufficient Balance Policy

If your wallet balance is lower than the daily repayment amount on a given day, FlowFund does not take a partial payment and does not charge any late fee. Instead, the day's debit is recorded as "skipped" and the wallet balance is left unchanged.

Skipped payments are tracked in your transaction history. They do not immediately trigger a default, but a pattern of skipped debits is a strong signal that the loan is under stress and raises your risk score.

To recover from a skipped day, simply top up your wallet. The next day's debit will run normally and the missed payment is not retried automatically. This means you may end the loan with a shortfall — which we will flag in your schedule and recommend you cover via an early top-up.
