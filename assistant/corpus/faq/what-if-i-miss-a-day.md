# FAQ: What happens if I miss a day or two?

Question: What happens if I miss one or two daily debits?

Answer: Missing one or two debits is not a default and does not trigger any penalty or fee. The days are recorded as "skipped" in your transaction history, and your wallet balance is left unchanged on those days.

However, skipped debits are a signal that your wallet buffer is too thin. If you have a three-day buffer in your wallet at all times, a single slow day should not cause a skip at all. If skips are happening, the immediate action is to top up your wallet to at least three times your daily repayment amount.

A default is only triggered at three or more skipped debits cumulatively over the life of the loan. Even at that point the loan continues to run — but the risk engine now flags you as HIGH risk, and we strongly recommend rebuilding the buffer before the next debit runs.
