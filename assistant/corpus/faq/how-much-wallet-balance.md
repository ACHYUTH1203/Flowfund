# FAQ: How much should I keep in my wallet?

Question: What's the right amount to keep in my FlowFund wallet?

Answer: At minimum, three times your daily repayment amount. If your daily repayment is Rs 200, keep at least Rs 600 in the wallet at all times.

Three days is the threshold below which the risk engine flags you as MEDIUM risk. At fewer than one day of coverage, it flags HIGH and warns you cannot cover tomorrow's debit. The target of three days gives you a working margin for ordinary day-to-day income noise.

If your income is highly variable — a shop heavily dependent on weekends or on weather — consider keeping more. A five- or seven-day buffer is not excessive for someone whose income can drop 50 percent on a bad week.

The buffer is money in motion, not money frozen. You will use it on slow days and replenish it on busy days. The goal is for the wallet to never drop below the three-day mark at the end of any given day.
